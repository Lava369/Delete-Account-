Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. VaidyaCare does not collect or store any personal data unless explicitly required for providing healthcare services. We only use data necessary for the proper functioning of the app.

Data is used strictly for app functionality, healthcare services, and improvements. We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties except when legally required.

Users may request deletion of their data at any time. Upon request, all associated information will be permanently removed from our systems, subject to applicable laws and medical record requirements.
